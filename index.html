Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity safeguards our computers, networks, and data from malicious attacks. These attacks can be launched by people or programs with various goals, from stealing sensitive information to causing complete disruption. Firewalls, encryption, and other tools are used to create a secure barrier, ensuring only authorized users can access information. Cybersecurity also involves keeping software updated and having plans in place to quickly respond to attacks to minimize damage. Because new threats constantly emerge, cybersecurity is an ongoing process that requires everyone to be vigilant. By following good security practices, like using strong passwords and being cautious about online clicks, we can all play a role in protecting ourselves and others from cyberattacks.
